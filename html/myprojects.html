Whack a mole has been a very popular game for many years and is a great project for a junior web developer. Creating this was a challenging task but the secret was in breaking down the problem into many small problems and tackling them one by one rather than trying to solve the whole problem at one. You climb a mountain one step at a time, not by jumping from the bottom straight to the top. Inspired by love towards animals the game has a twist where instead of whacking the mole why dont we just feed it, after all it's a similar approach just a few minor changes? If you would like to try the game out click here and keep an eye our for the king mole as feeding him is worth double points!
